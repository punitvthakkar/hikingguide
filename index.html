Essential Tips

  • ⏰ Punctual Departure
    Hard start at 11 AM sharp to maximize daylight and trail experience
  • 🌤️ Weather Awareness
    Check forecast before departure; wet conditions make trails slippery
  • 💧 Stay Hydrated
    Bring minimum 1.5L water for the 3-hour duration
  • 🔋 Power Management
    Keep phone charged for GPS/emergency. Power bank strongly recommended
  • 👥 Group Safety
    Stay together at trail forks and communicate your location
  • 🦌 Wildlife Respect
    Observe animals from distance and avoid feeding
  • 🌿 Leave No Trace
    Pack out all trash and stay on marked paths
  • ⚠️ Personal Responsibility
    ESMT provides planning support. You're responsible for personal safety
